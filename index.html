About the Trust

Dedicated to Transforming Lives Across India

Athrav Education Group Trust is a registered charitable organization committed to the holistic development of underserved communities in India. Founded with a mission to bridge the gap in education, healthcare, and social welfare, we work tirelessly to create a more equitable and empowered society.

Our initiatives span across education access, free healthcare camps, skill development, scholarship programs and community welfare projects — all designed to uplift and empower individuals at the grassroots level.

🎯 Our Vision

A progressive India where every citizen has access to quality education and healthcare.

Free Health Check-up Camps

Regular camps across rural Bihar offering free consultation, BP/sugar screening, eye check-ups and essential medicines by qualified doctors.

Diagnostic Support

Subsidized blood tests, X-rays and other diagnostic services for families below the poverty line in collaboration with partner labs.
